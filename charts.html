Activation Tests

When a unit with weariness is selected to activate, roll a d10 and consult the table (or spend Vitae to bypass).

Weariness on unit D10 needed Vitae to bypass
0 No test needed —
1–2 4+ 1
3–4 6+ 2
5–6+ 8+ 3

Note: For each weariness beyond 6, the activation test thresholds and bypass costs do not change, but the Vitae cost to attempt to Strive increases by 1 per weariness beyond 6.

Strive

After a unit resolves its two initial actions, it may spend Vitae to attempt additional actions.

Strive attempt (this activation) Succeeds on Failure penalty
1st 4+ 1 weariness; activation ends
2nd 6+ 2 weariness; activation ends
3rd 8+ 3 weariness; activation ends
4th+ 8+ +1 additional weariness per extra attempt; activation ends
